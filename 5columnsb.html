Discovery

Star Trek Discovery stars Sonequa Martin-Green, Anthony Rapp, Mary Wiseman, Wilson Cruz, Michelle Yeoh and Doug Jones.

Mary Wiseman

Mary Wiseman plays acting first officer Ensign Sylvia Tilly. She also transforms into captain Killy when in the mirror universe.

Anthony Rapp

Plays Paul Stamets, science officer on the discovery. He combines physics and mycology into a fictional method for instantaneous faster than light travel.

Doug Jones

Science Officer and Lieutenant commanderon the Discovery Saru is a Kelpien, an alien prey species that lives in a perpetual state of fear.
